üìñ How to Use the Orchestrator

1

Export Agents from Main App

Analyze meetings in the main app, then click "Export Agent" to save each meeting as a .md file.

2

Upload Your Agents

Click "Add Agent Files" or drag & drop your .md agent files into the upload area.

3

Manage Your Knowledge Base

Toggle agents on/off, rename them, or remove them. Only active agents are used for queries.

4

Chat Across Meetings

Ask questions in the chat to find patterns, decisions, and action items across all your meetings.

5

Generate Cross-Meeting Insights

Click the button to get AI-generated insights: common themes, trends, risks, and recommendations.

  • Suggestion Chips: Click the quick query buttons for common cross-meeting questions.
